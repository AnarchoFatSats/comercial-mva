Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I hear back after contacting you?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our toll-free number for immediate assistance.

Do I need to have all my accident information ready when I contact you?

While having basic information about your accident is helpful, it's not required for initial contact. Our team can guide you through what information will be needed as we proceed with evaluating your case.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No, there is never a fee for contacting us or for the initial case evaluation. Our partner attorneys work on a contingency fee basis, meaning you only pay if they win your case.
