Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible if I was using my personal car for work?
Yes, if you were using your personal vehicle for work purposes at the time of the accident, you may be eligible for compensation beyond standard insurance coverage.
How much compensation could I qualify for?
Compensation varies based on factors like injury severity, medical expenses, lost wages, and liability. Work vehicle accidents often qualify for higher settlements due to commercial insurance policies and potential employer liability.
Do I need to pay anything upfront?
No. Our partner attorneys work on a contingency fee basis, meaning you pay nothing unless they win your case.
What if the accident was partially my fault?
Depending on your state's laws, you may still be eligible for compensation even if you were partially at fault. This is known as comparative negligence.
How long does a work vehicle accident claim take?
Most work vehicle accident claims are resolved within 6-18 months, depending on the complexity of the case, the parties involved, and whether workers' compensation is also a factor.
